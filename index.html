Who We Are

Bio Lab Medicine and Medical Devices Importer and Distributor is a trusted medical supplies wholesaler with over six years of experience supplying quality medical products to public and private hospitals across Ethiopia. We focus on closing healthcare supply gaps in underserved regions through ethical partnerships and quality service.

Our Mission: To become one of the top 10 medicine and medical device importers in Ethiopia by 2030, by building reliable, ethical, and scalable supply chains that improve healthcare access and quality—starting with underserved regions like Tigray, Amhara, and Afar.

Our Core Value: We are committed to providing high-quality medicines and medical devices at fair and competitive prices, ensuring healthcare providers can deliver safe, effective care without compromise.
